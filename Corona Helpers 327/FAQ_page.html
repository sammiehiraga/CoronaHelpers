FAQ

    1. Is the forum for both volunteers and those seeking aid? Yes. The forum is to make a post either offering or looking for help. You can connect with others in your area through direct messages to arrange actual meetings. 2. Does it cost money? No, if someone asks you for money please be advised that this is a strictly no cost volunteer service. Anyone requesting money will be removed. 3. How can I make sure i find people near me? This service is for the City of San Francisco. We recommend that you include your neighborhood in your post, or neighborhoods you would be willing to go to if you are a helper. This helps people seeking help find people close by. 4. Who can use the service? Anyone can use the service. This site doesn't have anything to do with actually recruiting people, it is simply a place where people can connect. 5. Who started this forum? A group of San Francisco State Students who were looking for a way to help during the Coronavirus pandemic.
